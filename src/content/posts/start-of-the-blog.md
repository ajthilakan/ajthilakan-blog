#### It Begins...

I've been meaning to do this for a while.

I am starting this blog as an experiment to get me writing more, and an effort to slightly shift the ratio of how much meaningful content I produce to how much I consume.

I enjoy reading and discussing anything under the umbrella of technology, product management & strategy – which is what this blog will be most focused on. However I do have other interests, so you can understand if I tangent into anything ranging from gaming to the creative arts, or life itself. When it comes to the craft of product management, I do feel the urge to share the learnings from my journey along this winding path, with the hopes that I can contribute back to the ever evolving practice.

While I am still finding my voice, my style might initially be influenced by some smart folks I enjoy reading (e.g. [Ben Thompson](https://stratechery.com/about/), [Byrne Hobart](https://diff.substack.com/about), [Eugene Wei](https://www.eugenewei.com/info), and [Sachin Rekhi](https://www.sachinrekhi.com/) among many others) and my other areas of interest (I'm a long time creeper on [YCnews](http://news.ycombinator.com/), and subscribe to various newsletters in the world of technology and finance).

In an effort to start building momentum, I will be aiming to post regular bite sized reflections on recent news and topics I have been following. And as this progresses, I hope I can also write longer form pieces at less frequent intervals.

The cadence will vary.
